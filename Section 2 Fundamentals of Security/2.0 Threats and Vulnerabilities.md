### ğŸ”¹ 3. Risk Management Decisions

Cybersecurity professionals deal with vulnerabilities by choosing one of four options:

|Option|Description|
|---|---|
|**Mitigate**|Take action to reduce or eliminate risk (e.g., patching, firewall configuration)|
|**Transfer**|Shift the risk to another entity (e.g., insurance, outsourcing)|
|**Avoid**|Stop the risky activity altogether|
|**Accept**|Acknowledge the risk and decide to tolerate it (low-priority risk)|
