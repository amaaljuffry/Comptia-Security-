#### 3Ô∏è‚É£ **Operational Controls**

- **Definition:** Procedures and measures executed **day-to-day** by people to protect data and systems.

- **Examples:**
    
    - Password rotation (e.g., every 90 days)
    - Backup procedures
    - Account reviews
    - User awareness training
- **Key idea:** Human-driven, routine security practices.

#### 4Ô∏è‚É£ **Physical Controls**

- **Definition:** Tangible, real-world measures to **protect assets from physical threats**.

    - Surveillance cameras
    - Locked doors
    - Security guards
    - Secure shredding of documents
- **Key idea:** Physical barriers to protect assets and facilities.
