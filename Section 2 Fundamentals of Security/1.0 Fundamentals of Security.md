### ğŸ§  **3. Core Security Definitions**

#### **Information Security**

> Protecting the **data and information** from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.  
> **Focus:** The _data_ itself.  
> **Example:** Encrypting files, protecting customer records.

#### **Information System Security**

> Protecting the **systems** that store, process, or transmit data.  
> **Focus:** The _devices or infrastructure_.  
> **Example:** Securing servers, networks, or smartphones.
